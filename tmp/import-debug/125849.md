GMK CYL Windbreaker
GB Live Until November 9th!
It&#039;s here folks. After 3 years, it&#039;s finally time for GMK CYL Windbreaker. Sorry I&#039;ve made you wait longer than I should have, but thank you for your patience, and I hope you enjoy the set. I&#039;ve made you wait long enough, so on with the show!
Kits
Base
A phat base kit for almost everything you could need to cover: TKL, 65%, 60%, Alice, 4-key 40%, HHKB and more!
Novelties
Some choice novelties referencing some of your fave goodies of the past, also features orange gamer WASD.
Numpad
Wanna crunch some numbers? Here you go.
International
A little something something for my ISO friends.
Pricing
US Pricing
Base: $135
Novelties: $45
Numpad: $35
International: $65
Vendors
Worldwide: Proto[Typist]
US: Novelkeys
EU: Oblotzky Industries
KR: Geon
CN: Typist Club
SEA: iLumKB
Renders
Colors
GMK will be sent references from DCS Windbreaker for colormatching purposes.
Details
GB runs from October 10th 2025 to November 9th 2025.
Cherry Profile, ABS, Doubleshot, MX compatible, 112344 row sculpt, manufactured by GMK, you know the drill folks.
Estimated Shipping: Q1-Q2 2026
Thanks
Thank you to everyone who helped get me off my ass and work on this, thanks to everyone who kept asking for this over the last few years, and thanks to all of you who helped make the original DCS Windbreaker a reality.
Who would have thought an April Fools joke could go on this long?
Join My Discord or follow me on Instagram if you&#039;re into that sort of thing.
Thanks for looking
